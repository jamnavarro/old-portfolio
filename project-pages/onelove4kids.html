One Love 4 Kids Website UI/UX Redesign
Timeline
September 2022 - Present
Medium & Tools
FigJam Whiteboard, Figma, Maze, Google Forms
Role and Responsibilities
I am working in a team as Scheduling Manager and contribute in conducting stakeholder interviews, user research and interviews, wireframing, and prototyping.
Project Background and Overview
As an active member of Johns Hopkins' JHUXplore Club, I had the opportunity to work in a team with fellow aspiring designers and researchers on a website redesign project for the Washington DC and Kingston, Jamaica-based non-profit organization, One Love 4 Kids. One Love 4 Kids aims to provide educational, health, and economic support for children and their families in these areas to help them succeed in life.
What's the Goal?
We hoped to conduct a full UI and UX redesign of the One Love 4 Kids website to make it easier to learn about the organization and offered events, give donations, and sign up to volunteer.
